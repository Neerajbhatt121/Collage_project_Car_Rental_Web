Welcome to our Car Rental System!
To ensure a smooth and secure experience for all our users, we have established the following policies:
Reservation and Cancellation: Reservations can be made in advance, and cancellations can be processed with a notice period. Refer to our reservation and cancellation policy for details.
Driver's License: All renters must possess a valid driver's license. International renters are required to provide an international driving permit along with their original license.
Age Requirement: Renters must be at least 21 years old to book a car. Additional charges may apply for drivers under 25.
Insurance Coverage: Comprehensive insurance coverage is provided, but renters are encouraged to review the terms and conditions. Additional coverage options are available for those seeking extra protection.
Fuel Policy: Vehicles are rented with a full tank of fuel and should be returned in the same condition. A refueling charge will apply if returned with less fuel.
